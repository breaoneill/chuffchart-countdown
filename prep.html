Pre-Surgery Diet & Exercise Plan

🕒 Fasting

16:8 intermittent fasting (first meal at ~12:30, last meal by 20:30)

Hydration: herbal teas (rooibos, fennel), water – no calories outside eating window

🥗 Bento Box Ideas
  • Kimchi + rice cakes + cashews or cheese
  • Celery & hummus (cold pack)
  • Protein: grilled chicken, boiled eggs, pancetta bites
  • Miso broth in flask (or hot stock cube)
🏃 Exercise Routine
  • Short walks before or after work
  • Stretching + light core each morning
  • Resistance bands or light dumbbells twice a week
🧘 Recovery Prep
  • Low-residue meals 48 hours before surgery
  • Electrolytes and calm hydration the day before
  • Checklist: bag packed, paperwork ready, mindset steady

🥬 Sil’s Kimchi – October 2025 Edition

Yield: ~6kg

Vegetables

  • 5 Napa cabbages – chopped into 2–3" pieces
  • 1 small celeriac – julienned
  • 1 carrot – julienned
  • Spring onions – 2–3" strips
  • 1 Conference pear – grated
  • 1 medium onion – grated

Spice Paste

  • 1–1.5 cups gochugaru
  • 2–3 tbsp gochujang
  • 3 tbsp white miso
  • 2–3 anchovy fillets (mashed)
  • 1–2 tsp dasida
  • ½ cup garlic (minced)
  • ¼ cup ginger (minced)
  • 1 pear + 1 onion (grated/blended)

Instructions

  1. Salt cabbage, celeriac, carrot with ½ cup salt. Rest 6–8 hrs. Rinse & drain.
  2. Mix paste. Adjust spice to taste.
  3. Massage paste into veg. Add spring onions. Coat well.
  4. Pack into a clean plastic box. Press firmly. Leave 2–3 cm space at top.

Fermentation

  • Room temp: 2–3 days (burp daily)
  • Refrigerate for 4+ weeks. Improves over time.

Tips

  • Use a clean weight or loose lid
  • Expect bubbles and brine early
  • Lasts 2–3 months refrigerated
