Pre-Surgery Diet & Exercise Plan

🕰️ Fasting

  • 16:8 intermittent fasting (first meal ~12:30, last meal by 20:30)
  • Hydration: plain water, herbal teas (rooibos, fennel), black tea or black coffee – no milk, sugar, or sweeteners

🥗 Bento Box Ideas

  • Kimchi + rice cakes + cashews or cheese
  • Celery & hummus (cold pack)
  • Protein: grilled chicken, boiled eggs, pancetta bites
  • Miso broth in flask (or hot stock cube)

🏃 Exercise Routine

  • Short walks before or after work
  • Stretching + light core each morning
  • Resistance bands or light dumbbells twice a week

🧘 Recovery Prep

  • Low-residue meals 48 hours before surgery
  • Electrolytes and calm hydration the day before
  • Checklist: bag packed, paperwork ready, mindset steady

🌶️ Kimchi Corner

Your homemade kimchi is now over 2 weeks old and refrigerated. Use sparingly in bento, soups, or with rice cakes. Check for taste, fizz, and signs of over-fermenting.

  • It's still alive, just like you.
  • Add to bento box as a flavour hit
  • Stir into broth or noodle bowls
  • Eat chilled on rice cakes with sliced boiled egg

🥬 Sil’s Kimchi – October 2025 Edition

Yield: ~6kg

Ferment time: 2–3 days room temp, then 4+ weeks in fridge

🧂 Vegetables

  • 5 Napa cabbages – quartered, cored, chopped into 2–3" pieces
  • 1 small celeriac – peeled and julienned
  • 1 carrot – julienned
  • Spring onions – cut into 2–3" strips
  • 1 Conference pear – grated
  • 1 medium onion – grated or blended

🌶️ Spice Paste

  • 1 to 1.5 cups gochugaru
  • 2–3 tbsp gochujang
  • 3 tbsp white miso
  • 2–3 anchovy fillets (in oil), mashed finely
  • 1–2 tsp dasida (beef stock powder)
  • ½ cup fresh garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 grated pear and 1 grated/blended onion
  • Optional: up to ½ cup water to loosen paste
