Pre-Op Toolkit

This is my focused plan to support weight loss, energy, and recovery before gender-affirming surgery. Built on fasting, functional food, and movement.

Fasting Routine

Method: Intermittent fasting (14:10 or 16:8)

  • No food before 10:30am (or noon on stricter days)
  • Hydration: water, black coffee, herbal tea
  • First meal = protein priority

Meal Planning

Workday Meal Ideas (Microwave / Toaster / Kettle)
  • Egg muffins with spinach & cheese
  • Soup + protein pot (boiled egg, tuna, chicken)
  • Toasted wrap with hummus & greens
  • Greek yoghurt + berries + oats
Evening Meals (Cooked at home)
  • Veg stir-fry with chicken or tofu
  • Baked fish + roast veg
  • Turkey bolognese + courgetti

Movement

  • 15–30 min daily walk
  • Light resistance (chair squats, wall pushups, bands)
  • Stretch or floor work 1–2 times a week
