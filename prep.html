Pre-Op Toolkit

This is my focused plan to support weight loss, energy, and recovery before gender-affirming surgery. Built on fasting, functional food, and movement.

Fasting Routine

Method: Intermittent fasting (14:10 or 16:8)

  • No food before 10:30am (or noon on stricter days)
  • Hydration: water, black coffee, herbal tea
  • First meal = protein priority

Meal Planning

Workday Meal Ideas (Microwave / Toaster / Kettle)
  • Egg muffins with spinach & cheese
  • Soup + protein pot (boiled egg, tuna, chicken)
  • Toasted wrap with hummus & greens
  • Greek yoghurt + berries + oats
  • Rice cakes with Marmite or hummus
  • Celery sticks and a small portion of cashews
Evening Meals (Cooked at home)
  • Veg stir-fry with chicken or tofu
  • Baked fish + roast veg
  • Turkey bolognese + courgetti

Bento Box Plan (4-Day Work Week)

Designed for prep on Sundays and quick grab-and-go nutrition:

Monday – Miso Chickpea Bowl
  • Boiled egg + hummus
  • Steamed broccoli, radish, or kimchi
  • Brown rice with soy or miso drizzle
  • 8–10 cashews
Tuesday – Chicken & Crunch
  • Roast chicken slices
  • Celery sticks + red pepper
  • 2 rice cakes (with Marmite or hummus)
  • Optional: kimchi tub
Wednesday – Mediterranean
  • Tuna with lemon + black pepper
  • Spinach, olives, tomatoes
  • ½ cup couscous or bulgur
  • Handful of cashews or a piece of fruit
Thursday – Tofu Noodle Pot
  • Tofu cubes (or turkey)
  • Pak choi or cabbage + spring onion
  • Rice noodles
  • Kimchi on the side

Kimchi Corner

Home-made kimchi is a gut-friendly, low-calorie flavour bomb. Use 2–4 tbsp per bento or meal. Ideas:

  • Add to rice bowls or miso broth
  • Mix into noodles after reheating
  • Use with eggs, tofu, or hummus
  • Surprisingly good with Greek yoghurt for a spicy dip

Movement

  • 15–30 min daily walk
  • Light resistance (chair squats, wall pushups, bands)
  • Stretch or floor work 1–2 times a week
